Ideation

During the brainstorming process and figuring out what issue many people face daily, we decided to focus on back posture while sitting or standing. As we settled on the idea of back posture, we began to think about how back posture will be detected and measured with the tools we had available. After narrowing down to two different techniques for posture detection, an Elevator (Coffee-run) Pitch was given by a member on the team to the rest of the members and a third party group to discuss the technique they think will be best for the goal. The final decision was made as a group after hearing all the Elevator Pitches.
